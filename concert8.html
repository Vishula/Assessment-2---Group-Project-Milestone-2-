Townsville 150 Lets Celebrate

The concert is part of Townsville’s 150th anniversary celebrations. It will feature a variety of homegrown talent, including ARIA Award-winning performer DavidCampbell, Dirty Dancing musical star Kurt Phelan, The Voice Australia’s Luke Kennedy and many more. The soundtrack to the evening will be music performed by about 50 musicians made up of the Band of the First Battalion Royal Australian Regiment (1RAR) and the Barrier Reef Orchestra.

Conductors

Major Glen Rogers

Combined Barrier Reef Orchestra and 1RAR Band with Kurt Phelan, Luke Kennedy, Jade O’Halloran, Greta Sherriff, Bronwyn Douglass, (winner 2015 Australian Concerto and Vocal Competition Open Vocal section) David Campbell and Townsville Choral Society and NQOMT combined ensembles. (also featuring DanceNorth and Ulysses Dance)
