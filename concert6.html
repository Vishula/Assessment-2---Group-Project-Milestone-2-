Dreams of Dances

Don’t miss this one! We’re performing works from Saint Saens, Marquez, Faure, Debussy and Franck.

Conductors

Richard McIntyre

Faure: Pavane in F sharp minor Op. 50
Debussy: L’Apres-midi d’un Faune
Arturo Marquez: Danzon No. 2
Franck: Symphony in D minor
